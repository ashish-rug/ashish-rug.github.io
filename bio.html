About Me

I am an Associate Professor at the University of Groningen, where I am affiliated with the Engineering and Technology Institute Groningen and a the Jan C. Willems Center for Systems and Control. Before joining Groningen, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Automatic Control Laboratory at ETH Zürich, working with John Lygeros. I received my Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of California, San Diego (advisor: Jorge Cortés), an M.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering from ETH Zürich (2010), and a B.Tech. in Mechanical Engineering from IIT Delhi (2008). I served in the Conference Editorial Board of the IEEE Control Systems Society (2019-2023) and I currently serve as the Associate Editor of IEEE Control Systems Letters (2024-present).
