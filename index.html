Research interests

Broadly, I am interested in optimization, optimization-based control, and game theory in the context of networked cyber-physical systems, specifically related to energy and transportation. Currently I explore the following topics:

  • Data-driven stochastic optimization for energy networks

  • Distributed algorithms for network optimization problems

  • Routing games under uncertainty

  • Risk-averse model predictive control
